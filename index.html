Projects

Here are some of the personal projects I’ve done during my free time & some projects from OAMK.

Logging thermal sensor for solar panels with Raspberry Pi

During the 2nd year at OUAS, one of my friends was researching the thermal behaviour of solar panels and that could the heat captured by the panels be used in some way. For this we needed to capture the temperature of the panel & surrounding air for an extended period of time. I wrote a piece of software to measure & log temperature from 6 different sensors placed on & near the panels.

Media Centre

A simple media center & NAS setup made with a Raspberry Pi 3B, external HDD & wireless USB 2.4 GHz remote. The Pi runs DietPi Linux distribution, which is just a lightweight fork of Debian for ARM and on top of that there is Kodi, Pi-Hole, SFTP & local web server. I’ve been using this setup for a couple of years now without any problems

“Meme generator"
